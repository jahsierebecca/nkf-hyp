Kidney Health for Kids!

What your kidneys do for you:

Kidneys are very important to our bodies. They’re like the “washing machines” of our bodies. They make sure the waste is removed from our bodies, so our blood is nice and clean. Here in Hawaii, many people have sick kidneys. That’s why I want to teach you about your kidneys and teach you how to live a healthy lifestyle.

  • Cleans waste from the blood and filters the waste from the blood into urine
  • Keeps blood pressure normal
  • Balances all the fluids in the body
  • Produces hormones and Vitamin D to keep your bones and muscles strong!

What you can do for your kidneys:

Eat good food

Drink lots of water

Get lots of exercise

Water

Drinking water might be one of the best and easiest ways to help your kidneys! Fresh, clean water helps your kidneys filter the blood and remove the waster quickly... because what happens when you drink lots of water?? How much water do we need then?

Food

In order to keep your kidneys healthy, you should limit salt, fat and sugar in your diet. These items are okay in moderation, but you should be aware of how much sugar and salt is in your food, and find healthy alternatives to overly salty, fatty and sugary foods.

Exercise

Excersize is very important for growing bodies.

  • Helps you pay attention and remember things
  • Reduces stress
  • Gives you energy
  • Helps you sleep at night
  • Helps control weight
  • Helps bones and muscles grow and get strong
  • Helps your blood flow through your body
  • Decreases risk of diseases, like kidney disease
